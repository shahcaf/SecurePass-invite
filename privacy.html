Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

At SecurePass, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the SecurePass Discord bot and website.

1. Data We Collect

SecurePass collects only the minimum data necessary to provide verification services:

  • Discord User IDs: To identify users and manage verification status.
  • Guild (Server) IDs: To store server-specific configurations and logs.
  • Role IDs: To grant access to verified users.
  • Usage Logs: Verification timestamps and attempt results for security auditing.

2. How We Use Data

The information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Automating the verification process.
  • Preventing bot raids and malicious activity.
  • Providing server administrators with verification logs.

3. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely using CockroachDB. We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information. We do not store personal identifiable information (PII) beyond what Discord provides for bot functionality.

4. Third-Party Sharing

SecurePass does not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Data is only accessed by the bot's automated systems to fulfill its purpose.

5. Data Retention

We retain verification logs for a limited period (typically 30 days) unless requested otherwise by the server administrator. Users can request data deletion by contacting us or by having the server administrator clear logs.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your privacy, please reach out via our official support channels or via Discord.
